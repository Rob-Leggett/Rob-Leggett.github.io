What does certificate management do?

it lets your connecting users and services know that it is safe to connect to your service, you have a verified status, as well as supporting encryption controls in place.

What is Certificate Management?

Certificate management is the process of managing digital security certificates. This includes processes such as:

*   Creation
*   Storage
*   Dissemination
*   Suspension
*   Revocation

Certificate authorities are responsible for certificate management and serve as a registration authority for subscriber certificates.

A public key infrastructure (_PKI_) is a set of roles, policies, hardware, software and procedures needed to create, _manage_, distribute, use, store and revoke digital _certificates_ and _manage_ public-key encryption.
